Final Project

Welcome to the class projects page! In here, you will find a few larger projects and assignments I have worked on during ICS3U. They are all listed below with some reflections and comments regarding the difficulty, enjoyment and other obstacles resulting from the assignments.


Monty Hall Assignment (Extra Credit)
Here is an extra credit assignment that I completed throughout the JavaScript Unit. It relates to the show where a contestand must pick the right door to win a car, otherwise they get a goat. The code was not too complex, but it definately took me some problem solving and commitment.


Languages of the World
Here is an assignment where I had to team up with a partner to create a poster about the computer language of JavaScript. We covered the history, the uses, importance, comparisons, etc... This was a decent project and was great to learn about various languages.


History of Computers Website (HOC)
Here is an assignment were we had to create a website based on a historical figure who contributed to computers. I got to create a website based on the inventor of the first digital computer. It was a pretty rewarding project when I finished.


Careers In ICS
In this assignment, I created a video of myself talking about the opportunities of the careers in sales and marketing. I discussed how they relate to ICS and how these careers utilize technology to fit into the modern world and excel. This was a very enjoyable project to work on as I am very interested in business.
